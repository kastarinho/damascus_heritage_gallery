Featured Artwork

Discover the seasonal highlight capturing the essence of Damascus courtyard life

Morning Light in the Courtyard

Oil on Canvas • 1987 • 60 × 80 cm

This masterpiece captures the ethereal quality of Damascus morning light as it filters through traditional mashrabiya screens, illuminating the peaceful courtyard where generations have gathered for centuries.

Cultural Context

The courtyard represents the heart of traditional Damascus homes, where families would gather around fountains and orange trees, creating intimate spaces for daily life and celebration.

Preservation Stories

Discover the ongoing efforts to conserve and preserve these precious cultural artifacts

Conservation Efforts

Each artwork undergoes meticulous conservation using traditional techniques combined with modern preservation methods. Our team works with international experts to ensure these cultural treasures survive for future generations.

"Every brushstroke tells a story of Damascus. Our mission is to preserve not just the paint and canvas, but the cultural memory embedded within each piece."

— Heritage Conservation Team

Family Memories

Beyond the artworks themselves, we preserve the stories, memories, and cultural context that give each piece its deeper meaning. These personal narratives connect the art to the lived experience of Damascus families.

"I remember watching my grandfather paint in the courtyard every morning, the scent of jasmine mixing with oil paints. These paintings hold our family's soul."

— Family Member
