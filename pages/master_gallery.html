Morning Light in the Courtyard

Oil on Canvas • 1987 • 60 × 80 cm

Description

This masterpiece captures the ethereal quality of Damascus morning light as it filters through traditional mashrabiya screens, illuminating the peaceful courtyard where generations have gathered for centuries.

Cultural Context

The courtyard represents the heart of traditional Damascus homes, where families would gather around fountains and orange trees, creating intimate spaces for daily life and celebration.

Technique

Executed using traditional oil painting techniques with careful attention to light and shadow, demonstrating the artist's mastery of classical European methods adapted to Middle Eastern subjects.
