Journey Through Time

Explore the artist's life journey mapped against Damascus's rich cultural history, revealing how personal artistic development intertwined with broader cultural movements.

Navigate Timeline

1952 - 2010

1952

Birth in Damascus

Born in the historic Straight Street quarter, surrounded by centuries of artistic tradition.

1970

Art School

Enrolled at Damascus Fine Arts Academy, studying traditional techniques and modern approaches.

1975

First Solo Exhibition

Debut exhibition at Damascus Cultural Center featuring 25 paintings of traditional courtyard life.

1987

Masterpiece Period

Created his most celebrated works, including "Morning Light in the Courtyard."

1995

Cultural Recognition

Received Damascus Cultural Heritage Award for preserving traditional artistic techniques.

1960s

Cultural Renaissance

Damascus experiences a cultural renaissance with new art galleries and cultural institutions.

1979

UNESCO Heritage

Damascus Old City designated as UNESCO World Heritage Site, emphasizing cultural preservation.

1990s

Cultural Growth

Expansion of cultural institutions and art education programs throughout Damascus.
